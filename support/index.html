Support

We're here to help with AirGameBox and any other Xevigo app.

Get in touch

Fill in the form and choose how you'd like to send it. Your message opens in your email — pre-filled and ready — so nothing is sent until you review it and hit send yourself. We typically reply within 1–2 business days.

Prefer to write directly? Email support@xevigo.com. We don't store anything you type here — see our Privacy Policy.

Frequently asked questions

Is AirGameBox free?

Yes, AirGameBox is free to download from the App Store.

Do I need an account to play?

No. AirGameBox does not require registration or sign-in. Just download the app and start playing.

Does AirGameBox work offline?

Yes. AirGameBox is designed to work without an internet connection, so you can play anywhere.

Will my game progress be saved?

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Note that uninstalling the app will remove locally stored progress.

The app crashes or won't open. What should I do?

Try these steps: (1) close and reopen the app, (2) restart your device, (3) make sure you're on the latest version of the app and iOS. If the problem continues, email us at support@xevigo.com with your device model and iOS version.

How is my data handled?

AirGameBox does not collect personal information for gameplay. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Suggestions welcome
