Frequently asked questions

常见问题

Is AirGameBox free?AirGameBox 收费吗？

Yes, AirGameBox is free to download from the App Store.

AirGameBox 可在 App Store 免费下载。

Do I need an account to play?需要账号才能玩吗？

No. AirGameBox does not require registration or sign-in. Just download the app and start playing.

不需要。AirGameBox 无需注册或登录，下载后即可开玩。

Does AirGameBox work offline?AirGameBox 可以离线使用吗？

Yes. AirGameBox is designed to work without an internet connection, so you can play anywhere.

可以。AirGameBox 无需联网即可游玩，随时随地都能玩。

Will my game progress be saved?我的游戏进度会被保存吗？

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Note that uninstalling the app will remove locally stored progress.

游戏进度和设置会保存在你的设备本地。请注意，卸载应用会清除本地保存的进度。

The app crashes or won't open. What should I do?应用崩溃或打不开，怎么办？

Try these steps: (1) close and reopen the app, (2) restart your device, (3) make sure you're on the latest version of the app and iOS. If the problem continues, email us at support@xevigo.com with your device model and iOS version.

请尝试以下步骤：(1) 关闭并重新打开应用，(2) 重启设备，(3) 确认应用和 iOS 都已更新到最新版本。若问题依旧，请发邮件至 support@xevigo.com，并附上你的设备型号和 iOS 版本。

How is my data handled?我的数据如何处理？

AirGameBox does not collect personal information for gameplay. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

AirGameBox 不会为游戏收集个人信息。完整说明请查看我们的隐私政策。

Suggestions welcome

欢迎提出建议

Have an idea for a new mini-game or a feature you'd love to see? We build AirGameBox for players like you — tell us at support@xevigo.com.

对新的迷你游戏或想要的功能有想法吗？AirGameBox 正是为像你这样的玩家打造的——欢迎告诉我们：support@xevigo.com。
